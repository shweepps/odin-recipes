classic Spaghetti Carbonara

Indulge in the timeless Italian classic, Spaghetti Carbonara

with this easy-to-follow recipe. With its creamy egg and cheese sauce, crisp bacon, and perfectly cooked pasta, this dish is the epitome of comfort food. It's a quick and satisfying meal that will transport your taste buds straight to Rome.

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 4 ounces pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

Steps Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain and reserve 1/2 cup of pasta water.
  2. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese. Season with black pepper.
  3. In a skillet, cook the diced pancetta or guanciale over medium heat until it becomes crispy. Add minced garlic and saut√© for another minute.
  4. Reduce heat to low, then add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet. Toss to combine the pasta and pancetta.
  5. Remove the skillet from heat, and quickly pour the egg and cheese mixture over the hot pasta. Stir vigorously to create a creamy sauce. If needed, add reserved pasta water to achieve the desired consistency.
  6. Garnish with fresh chopped parsley and extra Pecorino Romano cheese if desired.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy this Italian classic!
